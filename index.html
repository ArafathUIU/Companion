FAQs

Frequently asked questions

CompanionX uses zero-knowledge architecture, meaning your data is encrypted before it leaves your device. We don't store personal information—only anonymized interaction patterns that can't be traced back to you. All communications are end-to-end encrypted, and we never require identifying details like your name, email, or phone number.

Our AI chatbot is designed to provide mental health support, not replace professional therapy. It can offer immediate assistance, coping strategies, and mood tracking, but for clinical conditions, we recommend pairing it with our anonymous human counseling service. The AI can help determine when professional help might be beneficial.

Due to our privacy-first approach, we have no way to recover lost anonymous IDs or passwords. We recommend saving your login details in a secure password manager. If lost, you'll need to create a new account—this ensures no one can ever claim to recover your account on your behalf, protecting your anonymity.
